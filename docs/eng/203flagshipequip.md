# Flagship Equipment Crafting

You can craft Flagship equipment with blueprints and crafting materials.<br>
Crafting slots can be unlocked by using crystals.

![](http://d3bbxo4nelobc3.cloudfront.net/html/img/help/203_01slot.jpg)

<br>

### 1) Select Blueprint

Select the blueprint of the equipment you wish to craft. A higher grade blueprint will likely yield higher grade equipment.<br>The amount of material consumption will be decided by the grade of the blueprint.

![](http://d3bbxo4nelobc3.cloudfront.net/html/img/help/203_02blueprint.jpg)

<br>

### 2) Select Materials

Select the materials needed for crafting. The type of materials used will depend on the location of the equipment you wish to craft.<br>The higher the grade of the materials used, the higher the grade of the crafted equipment.<br>
Only for T4 flagship equipment crafting blueprints with a crafting rate of 100% the material selection box is immediately available in the material selection pop-up when crafting equipment.

![](http://d3bbxo4nelobc3.cloudfront.net/html/img/help/203_03material.jpg)

<br>

### 3) Combine Materials

Combine lower grade materials to create a higher grade material.

![](http://d3bbxo4nelobc3.cloudfront.net/html/img/help/203_04combine.jpg)

<br>

### 4) Multiple Crafting Option

By setting the number to be crafted, you can run multiple crafting tasks.

![](http://d3bbxo4nelobc3.cloudfront.net/html/img/help/203_06multi.jpg)

<br>

### 5) Crafting Completion

You can acquire the new equipment by selecting the crafted equipment through the crafting slot.<br>
On the craft completion screen, you can change the equipment's lock status.

![](http://d3bbxo4nelobc3.cloudfront.net/html/img/help/203_05result.jpg)
