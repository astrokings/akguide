# Combat

## Spacetime Wormhole Combat


![](https://astrokings.s3.ap-northeast-2.amazonaws.com/html/img/help/2103_01.jpg)

Once you enter the Spacetime Wormhole, you cannot leave.

The combat lasts for 30 minutes and is divided into 3 rounds.<br>
Each round has a duration of 10 minutes.<br>
Strongholds are activated sequentially each round, along with special buffs.<br>

<br>
<br>
<br>


## Ranking

Ranking will be decided according to the points acquired in the Wormhole<br>
Must acquire at least 500 event points to be counted in the ranking.

<br>
<br>
<br>


## Ship / Flagship Repair Support

![](https://astrokings.s3.ap-northeast-2.amazonaws.com/html/img/help/2103_02.jpg)

During the Spacetime Wormhole, no resources are consumed when repairing the ships or flagships.<br>
When the Wormhole ends, all ships and flagships damaged during the Federation War will be restored

<br>
<br>
<br>


## Planet Combat

You cannot attack other Commanders' planets in the Spacetime Wormhole.

<br>
<br>
<br>


## Scout

![](https://astrokings.s3.ap-northeast-2.amazonaws.com/html/img/help/2103_03.jpg)

You cannot scout other Commanders' planets in the Spacetime Wormhole.<br>
You can scout survey sites and strongholds, and each scout consumes 25 AP.

<br>
<br>
<br>


## Spacetime Pirates

![](https://astrokings.s3.ap-northeast-2.amazonaws.com/html/img/help/2103_04.jpg)

Spacetime pirates exist in the Spacetime Wormhole.<br>
Defeating these pirates consumes 3 AP and grants you Wormhole Points.

| Defeated | Wormhole Points | Platinum Wormhole Points |
| - | :-: | :-: |
| 1 | 10 | 50 |

<br>
<br>
<br>


## Survey Site

![](https://astrokings.s3.ap-northeast-2.amazonaws.com/html/img/help/2103_05.jpg)

Survey Sites exist in the Spacetime Wormhole.<br>
Occupying these sites grants you Wormhole Points, and you can earn additional points based on the duration of your occupation.

| Requirements | Upon Occupation | 30s | 60s | 90s | 120s | 150s | 180s |
| - | :-: | :-: | :-: | :-: | :-: | :-: | :-: |
| Wormhole Points | 20 | 100 | 160 | 280 | 460 | 700 | 1000 |

<br>
<br>
<br>


## Stronghold

![](https://astrokings.s3.ap-northeast-2.amazonaws.com/html/img/help/2103_06.jpg)

Only one stronghold can be occupied at a time.
Stronghold can be attacked by a rally, consisting of up to 4 fleets.

Occupying these strongholds grants you Wormhole Points, and you can earn additional points based on the duration of your occupation.<br>
Strongholds are open sequentially according to the round.

## Special Buff

Special buffs are applied each round.<br>
Each wormhole has its own specific buff values.

| Round | Buff info | Spacetime Wormhole - Low | Spacetime Wormhole - Mid | Spacetime Wormhole - High |
| - | :-: | :-: | :-: | :-: |
| 1 | Marching Speed | 100% | 50% | 25% |
| 2 | Marching Speed | 125% | 75% | 50% |
| 3 | Marching Speed | 175% | 125% | 100% |
