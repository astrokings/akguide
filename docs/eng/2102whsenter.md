# Spacetime Wormhole Entry

![](https://astrokings.s3.ap-northeast-2.amazonaws.com/html/img/help/2102_01.jpg)

The Spacetime Wormhole can be entered once a week, entry resets every Wednesday at 00:00 (UTC).
Furthermore, you can also enter the Wormhole that are already in progress.

<br>
<br>
<br>

## Spacetime Wormhole Entry Conditions

- No fleets must be deployed
- No flagships or ships must be undergoing repair
- No damaged flagships/ships

<br>
<br>
<br>

## Spacetime Wormhole Types

Wormholes will shift according to the Command Center’s Level.

Spacetime Wormhole – Low : Command Center Lv. 35 ~ 43 <br>
Spacetime Wormhole – Mid : Command Center Lv. 44 ~ 50(CL.4) <br>
Spacetime Wormhole – High : Command Center Lv. 50(CL.5) ~ Command Center Lv. 50(CL.15)

<br>
<br>
<br>

## Precautions for Entering the Spacetime Wormhole

Inventory usage is unavailable in the Spacetime Wormhole.<br>
Please check your Speed Boost items, Action Points, and Crystals before entering, as they will be used in the Spacetime Wormhole.<br>
